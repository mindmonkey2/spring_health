## Full Description
**Welcome to the ultimate fitness experience at Spring Health Studio.**
Elevate your gym journey from a simple routine to an engaging, gamified lifestyle. Manage your membership, track your progress, and compete with friends—all from one powerful app.

### Key Features
- **Real-Time Membership Tracking**: Never miss a beat with live status updates and expiry countdowns for your gym membership.
- **Lightning-Fast QR Check-in**: Skip the front desk queue! Scan your personalized QR code to instantly log your daily attendance.
- **Health Connect Integration**: Automatically sync your daily steps, burned calories, heart rate, and sleep data directly from your phone or wearables.
- **Live Workout Logger**: Record your sets, reps, and weights with a built-in live timer to optimize your rest periods.
- **Trainer Access & Diet Plans**: Get personalized workout routines and nutrition plans directly from Spring Health certified trainers.
- **Instant Online Renewals**: Renew your membership securely in seconds via UPI, Cards, or Netbanking. Access your complete payment history anytime.
- **Studio Announcements**: Stay updated with real-time push notifications for gym closures, special events, and new equipment.

### For Spring Health Members
Designed exclusively for the members of Spring Health Studio in Warangal and Hanamkonda. Log in seamlessly using your registered phone number via secure OTP. Your profile automatically syncs with the studio's database, giving you immediate access to your personalized dashboard.

### Gamification: Level Up Your Fitness!
Fitness is a game, and you're the main character.
- **Earn XP**: Gain Experience Points for every gym check-in, logged workout, and synced daily step goal.
- **Rank Up**: Progress through 6 exclusive tiers—climb from Rookie all the way to Legendary status!
- **Collect Badges**: Unlock unique achievement badges for hitting Personal Bests (PBs), maintaining workout streaks, and crushing monthly goals.
- **Leaderboard & Clash Challenges**: Compete against fellow members in your branch. Join intense "Clash" fitness challenges to prove who works the hardest and claim the top spot on the live leaderboard.

### Complete Fitness Tracking
Monitor your daily and weekly physical activity at a glance. Our Cyber-Organic dashboard visualizes your Health Connect data alongside your gym attendance, giving you a comprehensive 360-degree view of your health journey.

**Ready to transform your life? Download the Spring Health app now and step into the future of fitness!**

## Data Safety Declaration

### 1. Personal Information (Phone Number)
- **Collected via**: Firebase Auth / Firestore
- **Purpose**: App functionality, Account management, Developer communications.
- **Shared with Third Parties**: Yes (Google/Firebase for authentication SMS).
- **User Deletion**: Users can request data deletion.

### 2. Health & Fitness Data
- **Collected via**: Health Connect (Steps, Calories, Heart Rate, Distance, Sleep) & In-App (Workout Logs, Attendance).
- **Purpose**: App functionality (tracking progress, gamification XP, leaderboards).
- **Shared with Third Parties**: No.
- **User Deletion**: Users can request deletion. Data synced via Health Connect can also be managed/deleted directly from OS settings.

### 3. Personal Info (Name, Branch, Expiry Date) & Financial Info (Payment History)
- **Collected via**: Firestore / Razorpay
- **Purpose**: App functionality, Account management.
- **Shared with Third Parties**: Yes (Razorpay processes payments, Google/Firebase stores data).
- **User Deletion**: Users can request deletion (subject to Razorpay's financial data retention legal requirements).

### 4. Photos (Profile Image)
- **Collected via**: Firebase Storage
- **Purpose**: App functionality, Account management.
- **Shared with Third Parties**: No.
- **User Deletion**: Users can request deletion or overwrite the image.

### 5. Device or Other IDs (FCM Tokens)
- **Collected via**: Firebase Cloud Messaging
- **Purpose**: App functionality (Push Notifications).
- **Shared with Third Parties**: Yes (Google for delivery).
- **User Deletion**: Automatically invalidated on uninstall, or upon user request.
